## The Story

**Four generations. One game. Endless lessons.**

My sisters and I started on the diamond, just like those that came before us. We were lucky - we had guidance from our grandfather who played 13 years in the Major Leagues and went on to manage at the AAA level. Baseball was everything to him. I didn't fully appreciate what he was passing down until much later. It was never just about the game.

It's about what the game gives you.

The mental toughness. The discipline. How to respond when things don't go your way. Our family tree runs deep - one MLB player, my dad who was a standout wrestler, and nine college athletes across five different sports. Different paths, same mental foundation. It wasn't talent that connected us. It was mindset.

**Failure isn't the enemy. Quitting is.**

You have to fail to grow. It's how you respond that defines you. That's the lesson. That's what gets passed down.

Having kids changed everything. Suddenly I was on the other side - coaching, cheering, showing up the way my parents always did for me. That's when I finally understood what my grandfather knew all along: it's not about the trophies - it's about who you become chasing them. It was never just about baseball. It was about what baseball builds.

**That's why we built Mind & Muscle.**

Not to sit on the sidelines and tell you what to do. To get on the field with you. The mental side of the game isn't a nice-to-have - it's the difference maker. It's what separates those who fold from those who rise.

We built this for the athlete who refuses to settle. For the parent who shows up every single time. For the coach who pours everything into their team. We're not ahead of you. We're beside you. Still grinding. Still learning. Still pushing.

**Discipline the Mind. Dominate the Game.**

**Let's go.**
